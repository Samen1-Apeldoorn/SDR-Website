About Our Network

Our network of SDR (Software Defined Radio) listening stations monitors radio frequencies across the region. These stations help us gather data on signal coverage, interference patterns, and broadcast quality.

Each station is equipped with advanced antennas and digital receivers, allowing for precise monitoring and analysis of the radio spectrum.
